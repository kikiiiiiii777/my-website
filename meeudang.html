Mee Udang Warung Tok Mat

Mee Udang features succulent prawns served in a savory broth with noodles. The broth is a perfect balance of spicy and savory, with a burst of flavor from the fresh prawns. It's a must-try dish for seafood lovers in Penang.

Address: Jalan Tanjung Bunga, Tanjung Tokong, 11200 Tanjung Bungah, Penang.
